Keep it free. Keep it wild.

Every gift helps care for Willow Waterhole today while protecting and improving it for future generations.

Willow Waterhole is free for everyone, every day, and it stays that way because of people who care about this place. Your gift helps care for the park today while investing in its future through conservation, education, community programs, park improvements, and the everyday stewardship that keeps Willow Waterhole thriving.

Your Impact

Your Gift Makes Possible

Care for Nature

Restore native habitat, protect wildlife, improve wetlands, and preserve one of the last remaining coastal prairies in Texas.

Care for the Park

Maintain 8.5 miles of trails, lakes, bridges, trees, gathering places, and the everyday improvements that keep Willow Waterhole welcoming.

Connect the Community

Support educational programs, guided walks, community events, and opportunities that help people connect with nature.

Invest in the Future

Imagine More with Willow Waterhole

This year's Annual Fund helps care for Willow Waterhole every day while investing in new opportunities for the future. From maintaining beautiful public spaces to expanding programs and improving visitor experiences, your support helps the Conservancy do more for both nature and the community.

Park Care

More than 5,000 hours of mowing, habitat care, and beautification help keep trails and open spaces welcoming year-round.

Visitor Safety

Your gift provides 1,000–1,500 hours of live security presence, so families, walkers, birders, and school groups can explore confidently.

Help expand guided walks, volunteer days, markets, festivals, and hands-on nature programs that connect people with the land.
