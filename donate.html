Donate

Keep it free. Keep it wild.

Every gift restores prairie, plants native trees, and maintains the lakes and trails that make Willow Waterhole remarkable.

Willow Waterhole is free for everyone, every day, and it stays that way because of donors who believe in it. Your gift goes directly to the work of restoring habitat, maintaining eight miles of trails, and protecting one of the last coastal prairies in Texas.

Your Impact

What your gift does.

Restores the prairie

Supports the care of 15 acres of rare coastal prairie and the endangered and threatened species that live almost nowhere else.

Plants native trees

Funds tree plantings and habitat restoration that bring shade, beauty, and wildlife to the greenway.

Maintains the trails

Keeps eight miles of ADA trails, benches, signage, and gathering spaces safe and welcoming for all.

Welcomes everyone

Keeps the park free and open to thousands of visitors every year, no admission ever required.
