Willow Waterhole Featured in Texas Parks & Wildlife

A first for the Conservancy: Willow Waterhole's story is being shared with Texas's outdoor community in Texas Parks & Wildlife magazine, the state's flagship outdoors publication.

It's fitting recognition of a long partnership. The Texas Parks & Wildlife Department stocked four of the park's lakes with bass, catfish, and sunfish, placed the greenway on the Great Texas Coastal Birding Trail, and funded the new Prairie Boardwalk through a 2023 grant. Now the park built on that partnership is being introduced to readers across the state.

For a park long known as Houston's hidden gem, the secret is getting a little harder to keep, and we're glad. Come see what the fuss is about.
