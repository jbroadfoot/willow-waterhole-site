2025 · Park News

The Donald Perkins Trail Opens

A new trail now connects the heart of Westbury to the greenway, and it carries the name of a dedicated public servant and city planner: Donald Perkins.

Neighbors, family, and community leaders gathered for the dedication, a reminder that this park has always grown one connection at a time. Every trail at Willow Waterhole shortens the distance between a neighborhood and the outdoors, and this one brings the greenway closer to the community that championed it from the beginning.
